Welcome to England!

Amazing tourist attractions and more!

England is an infamous country. It has been around for a long time, and thus has alot of history! Therefore, the point of this webpage and our effort is to show you this history firsthand.

There are many places you could visit first, however a popular choice is the fabled Big Ben.
Big Ben is located in Westminster, London and is next to the River Thames.
A common misconception about Big Ben is that it is the tower itself. However, this is not true, as Big Ben is actually the clock within the tower!
